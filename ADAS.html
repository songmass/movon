MR77 is a mid-range 77G RADAR sensor, so the sensor's main role is to detect objects and to determine the distance, angle ( x & y value )and velocity of objects ahead of max.160 meter. And, we need to develop an ECU(Electronic Control Unit) or an embedded system to implement collision warning product or collision avoidance system of level 1 or 2 using the processed data from radar and vision sensor.

As you know, MDAS-9 alerts a driver of collision warning in impending dangerous situation based on vision sensor, MR-77plusMDAS-9 based on fusion sensors of RADAR and Vision is providing LDW, FCW and PCW even under the inclement weather(heavy rain,heavy fog,...) and at night time.
